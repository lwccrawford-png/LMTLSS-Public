The Purpose of Power
✍️ Lock This In
“Power is not given to elevate you — it’s given so you can elevate something else.”
Real power is not measured by how high you rise — it’s measured by what rises around you.
🧱 Dominion Defined
Dominion
Not domination. Not control. Dominion is not taking control — it’s taking responsibility.
  • →Dominion is the execution of legitimate, God-honoring power.
  • →Gaining power by any other means is destructive.
  • →If it’s not building, protecting, or growing — it’s not dominion.
⚠️ 3 Wrong Pursuits of Power
Validation
Chasing attention, recognition, being seen.
→ Power used for validation will empty you.
Pain
Climbing, winning, proving — driven by past hurt.
→ Power driven by pain will harden you.
Pride
More status, more control, more recognition.
→ Power fueled by pride will isolate you.
Bottom line: Power is not the problem. Misdirected power is the problem.
⚔️ Dominion Framework — Adam
God gave Adam unlimited power — with a specific purpose:
Work It
Build it
Keep It
Protect it
Multiply It
Grow it
Subdue It
Stand against opposition
That’s Dominion. Not control — Responsibility.
🔥 Power Reveals
“Nearly all men can stand adversity, but if you want to test a man’s character, give him power.”
— Abraham Lincoln
  • →Power doesn’t change you. It reveals you.
  • →It exposes what was already there.
